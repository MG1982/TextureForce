About

A small, family-run business with over 20 years experience. Dedication to workmanship, attention to detail and personal customer service have seen them develop into one of the industry’s largest top coating service providers in Western Australia. The company’s founders are heavily involved in the day-to-day operation of the company and still on the tools, so they've never lost the personal touch of a small business. Texture Force know their craft and bring a wealth of experience, care and expertise to every job.

Renovation

The street appeal of a house is half its value, and we can help you realise the potential in your home. We have teams specialising in facades and whole-house renders, to bring out the best in your house. Our experienced staff are trained to work on established sites without disturbing existing features like gardens and paving, and we try always to cause as little impact and inconvenience as possible..

Products & Services

Dulux Acratex

Dulux AcraTex® products provide a textured finish that protects against render cracking and staining, to keep homes looking great for longer. Call in to your nearest Dulux AcraTex display centre to find out more about AcraTex Performance Coatings for cement render.

Wattyl Granosite

Wattyl Granosite® has been leading specialist texture coating since 1952. Wattyl Granosite® offers the complete coating system that meets the challenges of evolving substrates and construction techniques.

STO

Renowned for having a crack resistance six to ten times more malleable than cement-based renders, Sto is an environmentally friendly, proven synthetic render system. ... “The product is also environmentally sensitive, reducing the harsh chemicals that are often used in other cladding systems.

Tufftex

With a choice of over 3,000 colours and a wide variety of application styles to choose from, finish off your project with TUFFTEX Acrylic Texture Coating and enjoy a minimum 7 year warranty.

ROCKCOTE

ROCKCOTE Textures add depth and dimension like no other wall coating. They are perfect for enhancing an architectural style, or evoking the natural beauty of sand, marble or stone. Designed for use under ROCKCOTE Armour Membrane Paint, they create attractive, tactile surfaces with character.

Dulux Venetian

The Venetian Plaster Collection blends traditional artisan craftsmanship with contemporary style to deliver truly distinctive and unique finishes suitable for both resident and commercial settings. Smooth and cool to the touch, the colours and finished crafted by hand make every wall an individual work of art.
