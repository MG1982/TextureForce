About

A small, family-run business with over 20 years experience. Dedication to workmanship, attention to detail and personal customer service have seen them develop into one of the industry’s largest top coating service providers in Western Australia. The company’s founders are heavily involved in the day-to-day operation of the company and still on the tools, so they've never lost the personal touch of a small business. Texture Force know their craft and bring a wealth of experience, care and expertise to every job.

Renovation

The street appeal of a house is half its value, and we can help you realise the potential in your home. We have teams specialising in facades and whole-house renders, to bring out the best in your house. Our experienced staff are trained to work on established sites without disturbing existing features like gardens and paving, and we try always to cause as little impact and inconvenience as possible.

Residential

We know there is always choice in the market and we work hard to distinguish ourselves from our competitors. Our professionalism and commitment sets us apart. Our passion for our work ensures we always go the extra mile, so that every client is not only satisfied but delighted with their project for many years to come.

Commercial

At the heart of our customer service model is the support we offer our clients. We are always available to provide assistance and advice. Our dedicated Customer Service, Repairs and Maintenance devision is able to take care of any issue that you may have or should arise with your project. We have been top coating for over 20 years – we know our industry, we respect our clients and we have built a broad specialist expertise that few can rival.
